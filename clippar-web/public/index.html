How it works
Click. Swing. Clip.
01

Click.

Clip your phone onto your buggy or bag. Tighten it, angle it how you like. Press the Bluetooth clicker on your belt before you walk up to the ball.

02

Swing.

Take your shot and pose for the camera. Your swing is captured automatically. Click when you're done.

03

Clip.

Our AI trims each clip to the perfect moment—pose, ball flight, impact. Walk off with a 5-minute highlight reel of your round, ready to share.

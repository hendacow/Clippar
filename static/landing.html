How it works
Three clicks.
A round of memories.
01

Clip on. Press go.

Mount your phone on the included holder. The Bluetooth clicker sits on your belt loop. Press once before each shot — that's all you ever do.

02

Play your round.

Forget the phone exists. Clippar's AI pipeline detects every shot from your raw footage — using pose estimation, ball flight tracking, and audio impact detection.

03

Walk off with a reel.

Every shot, trimmed and tagged. Add your score and Clippar assembles a PGA-style highlight video — music, overlays, ready to share.
